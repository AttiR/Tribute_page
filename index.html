Tribute Information

Education

Atta-ur-Rahman was born on 22 September 1942 in Delhi, British India (today's Republic of India) into an Urdu-speaking academic family. Atta-ur-Rahman was a bright student at school.

  • Bachelor's degree (with honors) in chemistry in 1964, with degree concentration in natural products.
  • Master of Science (MSc) in organic chemistry in 1965.
  • PHD: in Organic chemistry; his doctoral thesis contained fundamental work on natural products and organic materials

National & international awards

He has been honoured with many Nationa and international awards.

  • Nishan-e-Imtiaz (2002)
  • Hilal-e-Imtiaz (1998)
  • Sitara-e-Imtiaz (1991)
  • Tamgha-e-Imtiaz (1983)
  • Scientist of the Year Award (for 1985)
  • FPCCI Prize for Technological Innovation (1985)
  • TWAS Regional Prize for Institution Building (2009)
  • First Muslim scientist to receive the UNESCO Science Prize (1999)
